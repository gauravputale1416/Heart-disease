About Heart Disease Prediction

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. Early detection and risk assessment are crucial for prevention and timely treatment. Our web application uses machine learning to analyze key health indicators and provide personalized risk evaluations.

The model considers factors such as age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and other cardiac markers to deliver comprehensive health insights. While this tool provides valuable screening information, it should complement, not replace, professional medical advice and consultation.
